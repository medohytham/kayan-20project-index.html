About us

About kayan Flooring and Decoration
kayan is one of the leading companies in the field of flooring design and installation,
offering innovative and stylish solutions for commercial projects.
The company was established with the goal of transforming spaces into vibrant,
functional environments by providing a wide range of high-quality flooring options,
including vinyl flooring,
along with professional installation services that ensure complete customer satisfaction.
The company strives to offer integrated solutions that suit all tastes and budgets,
with a focus on the fine details that distinguish each project.
Thanks to a specialized team and modern machinery,
kayan guarantees the execution of projects on time and to the highest standards of quality.

About the Owner - Hytham omara is the founder and CEO of kayan,
with an extensive background in interior design and decoration.
Hytham omara began his professional career 15 years ago and has always sought to offer innovative services that reflect clients' tastes and meet their needs.
With his creative vision and commitment to quality,
he has successfully built a strong reputation for his company in both local and international markets.
Hytham omara has vast experience in interior consulting and design,
having contributed to many prestigious projects,
including the design and installation of flooring decorations for major national projects.
He believes in the importance of innovation and utilizing modern technology to achieve the highest standards of quality in all projects.
Under Hytham omara's wise leadership and commitment to excellence,
kayan has become one of the leading names in the industry,
earning the trust of clients across various sectors.
